{!-- Letter from Clark --}

Church,

It’s been a miraculous fiscal year! The numbers and graphics that follow paint a picture of the abundance of people who participate and serve in our house, the salvations and baptisms we’ve celebrated as a church family, your generous gifts and obedient tithes that mobilize our ministries and create environments where people meet with God, and so much more! It is amazing to see God’s hand at work and moving in this body of believers.

As the culture continues to blossom with distractions and the variance of the economy ebb and flow, we remain grounded in a consistent God whose love never fails and whom we believe can do anything. I pray that as you review our 2013-2014 annual report, you will celebrate with us the growth we’ve experienced, join us in prayer for our newest ministries, and anticipate God’s continued favor over our church in the coming years.

We have a great call, to transform cities one life at a time, and I am grateful, honored and humbled to see the Holy Spirit alive and people’s lives transformed through JourneyChurch. I believe the vision God has given us will continue to unfold and bear fruit as we honor Him, build healthy relationships and, of course, have a blast!


e3:20,

Clark Mitchell

Giving Options

MARKETABLE SECURITIES: TRANSFER OWNERSHIP OF STOCKS, BONDS OR MUTUAL FUND SHARES

Marketable Securities are a great way to maximize your giving because you may receive an immediate tax reduction for the full fair market value of your securities. In addition, you could pay no capital gains tax on the difference between your cost and fair market value.

OTHER NON - CASH GIFTS: DONATED ITEMS LIKE CARS, BOATS AND JEWELRY

Did you know that over 90% of the world’s wealth is held in assets, not cash? That means that most of your giving capacity is tied up in your “stuff,” not your checkbook. Contact the Ministry Services office at the church and we will work with you to determine the most appropriate way to donate your non-cash gift to the church. Donors will be sent an acknowledgement letter, including a description of the donated item and the date it was received.

LIFE INSURANCE: NAME JOURNEYCHURCH AS A BENEFICIARY OF YOUR POLICY

A current life insurance policy can be transferred to JourneyChurch, for which you will receive an immediate income tax deduction for the cash surrendered value. JourneyChurch will elect one of two options: cash in the policy for immediate ministry needs or keep it and utilize the death benefit later.

PLANNED GIFTS: GIVING RETIREMENT ASSETS, BEQUESTS, TRUSTS, ETC. AS PART OF YOUR OVERALL PERSONAL FINANCE STRATEGY

If you have planned in advance to give a gift as part of your overall personal finance strategy, we recommend that you discuss your decision with your tax and/or financial adviser. There are several types of gifts that may fall under this category, many of which are very simple to make and do not require any formal communication which include:

  • Life Insurance: you can simply name JourneyChurch as a beneficiary.
  • Retirement Assets: you can name JourneyChurch as a beneficiary of your IRA, 401(k) or other qualified retirement plan. Did you know that 50-60% of your retirement assets may be taxed if you leave them to your heirs at your death? Any amounts left in your plan will transfer to JourneyChurch free of income and estate tax.
  • Bequests: you can name JourneyChurch in your will as a beneficiary, and your estate will receive an income tax deduction for the value of your bequest. A bequest written in a will can be made for either a percentage or an actual amount.
    • Charitable Gift Annuity: cash or marketable securities are transferred to a charitable organization in exchange for a current income tax deduction and the organization’s promise to make fixed annual payments to you for life.
    • Charitable Lead Trust: cash or marketable securities are transferred to a trust, which makes fixed payments to the charitable organization over a specified period of time, then transfers the remaining assets to your heirs.
