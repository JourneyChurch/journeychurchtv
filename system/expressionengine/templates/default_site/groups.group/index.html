What is a Journey Group?

A Journey Group is a group of three or more people that gather to create friendships, grow in their faith and expand Our House.

How do I find a Journey Group?

To find the perfect group for you, stop by the “Connection Center” in the lobby or contact our groups team at groups@journeychurch.tv.

How do I lead a Journey Group?

To apply to lead a group, please take a few moments to share some information with us. Be sure to check the box marked "I would be interested in leading a group." We are so excited to serve alongside you!
