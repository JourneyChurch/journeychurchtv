How to use our household website

visit

Visit us online at household.JourneyChurch.tv and select the link "New Here? Sign Up!" Click on "Howdy (your name)" in the top right hand corner of the page.

 

edit

Click "Edit Profile" under your avatar to update your information and add a picture. Click "Save." Take time to view and edit the other categories under "Personal Information." Click "Menu" tab to close your Profile Settings.

 

edit

Click the "Me" tab to tailor your personal feed according to interest. Here you can "Add Apps" to provide spiritual growth and inspiration in the column on the left.

 

connect

Click the "Groups" tab at top of page to join your Life Group OR create a new group for people who share similar interested by clicking the "Groups" button at the top left hand corner of the page.

 

befriend

Click the "People" button next to the "Groups" button at the top left hand corner of the page to add friends.

 

community

Have fun connecting with others in our house through prayer, serving, and participating in our mobile community.

 

help?

Need some help? Connect with the Nerd Herd for expert assistance any time by emailing household@JourneyChurch.tv.

our house in your hands

Connect on the go with our household mobile app. Here you can watch previous messages, watch live, take live message notes, pray for one another, connect with friends, and customize your feed!

How to use our household app

download

Visit your mobile app store. Search "household". Download our household app.

 

sign up

Tap "Sign Up" and follow the prompt buttons OR "Sign In" (if you've already created a profile).

 

filter

Filter Your Feed >> Pull down on the screen and tap the “Filter” button. Select the area(s) of interest.

 

feed

Choose a Feed >> Tap the "JourneyChurch.tv" button at the top of the screen. Select any of your groups to keep up to date with their feed

 

teachings

Watch Teachings >> Tap on menu icon in top left hand corner. Tap "Teachings" in the list on the left

 

notes

Take Message Notes >> Tap on the Teaching you wish to watch > Tap “Watch” > Tap “Take Notes” > Tap “Insert Outline” > Add to the existing notes and click "Save" in the upper right hand corner

 

help?

Need more help? >> Tap “Feedback” in the menu listing for FAQ’s, video tutorials, and other useful tools. Or email household@JourneyChurch.tv.
