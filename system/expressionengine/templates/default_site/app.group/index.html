About


Thanks for your interest in the all-new Journey Church app! It’s been designed with you in mind in order to give you as many resources as possible to help your daily walk with Jesus and further connect you with our church. If you’re looking for a place to belong, you’ve found it.


We’ve highlighted a few of our favorite features for you to skim through and learn more about. From watching experiences to taking notes, and from weekly devotionals to online giving, we’ve got you covered.
