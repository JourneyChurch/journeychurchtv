Summary

The Fall Retreat is a weekend of worship, teaching, encountering God and having a blast!

The retreat begins with arrival/registration on Friday, Nov. 1 from 5-6pm, immediately followed by dinner. Retreat concludes on Sunday, Nov. 3 with lunch.


Transportation

Students are responsible to arrive at the retreat on their own. However, we are organizing a carpool that will be leaving from JourneyChurch's Norman Campus on Nov. 1 at 3:30pm. Please let us know during the registration process if you are able to be a carpool driver and/or if you need a ride to/from the retreat.


What to Bring

Below is a suggested packing list for the Fall Retreat. Throughout the weekend, there wil be "down time" where you're encouraged to play a game of football or basketball, go on a hike, sip hot chocolate on the front porch, or play cards. Pack accordingly!

  • Bible
  • Notebook
  • Pen
  • Journal
  • Toiletries
  • Towel
  • Pillow
  • Sleeping bag (or sheets for twin mattress)
  • Clothes for 2 days in fall weather
  • Clothes that you don't mind getting dirty
  • Clothes to sleep in
  • Jacket or Sweatshirt
  • Flashlight
  • Snack Shack Cash
  • Closed-toe shoes (for outdoor activity, walking/hiking, basketball, etc.)
