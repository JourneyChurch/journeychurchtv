About JourneyChurch

JourneyChurch is a house where God is honored, where prayers are answered, hearts are mended and lives are changed. We are a house committed to transforming our cities... one life at a time. A house that uses creativity to point people to the Creator. A house that believes the God of generations past is the answer for the next generation. The people of this house gather to build healthy relationships and have a blast together. And the highest calling of our house is to worship the God of the universe. The King of kings. The Lord of lords. We are a house. We are JourneyChurch.

Get Connected

JourneyChurch is a community filled with opportunity to get involved and connect with others. We'd love to help you get connected in a big way! We have created many different and unique connection opportunities, but the best way to engage in community quickly is to do these three things:

1.  Attend a worship experience each weekend.
2. Join a journey group
3. Sign up to serve
