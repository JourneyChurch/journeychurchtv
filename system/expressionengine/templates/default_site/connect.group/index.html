As followers of Christ, we must, like a tree, be planted with strongly connected roots to grow and be spiritually healthy. As part of the family at JourneyChurch, we are connected to God and each other as we serve, live in community, pray, give, and invite others to be a part of our church family.

At JourneyChurch, there are dozens of ways to get connected across all ages and stages of life. Your role is to choose from the many connection opportunities and take your first step of getting planted!

If you want help or more information, all you have to do is ask. At all locations, the Connection Center is a place for answers, assistance and more. Of course you can always email connect@JourneyChurch.tv or call as well. No matter the path, we are here to help you connect and get planted as we honor God, build healthy relationships and have a blast together!
