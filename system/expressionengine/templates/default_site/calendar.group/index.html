2013 was a BLAST! Together we have accomplished great things and seen lives transformed. With a fresh New Year in 2014, we are getting ready for a HUGE month. We hope that everyone can find a place here in the JourneyChurch family. We offer many opportunities for everyone to connect. Start the New Year off right and check out our calendar full of January events.

Your Opportunity
to Connect is HUGE!

January 2014 is bursting at the seams with opportunities for you to connect with God and others at JOURNEYCHURCH.TV. Find your opportunity below and let us help you get connected and start your year off right!



Awakening - January 8th

Begin the New Year by connecting with God. Awakening is a monthly event where we gather as a church family for an extended evening of worship, community and prayer. This month we have created a shared opportunity to worship God by fasting for one week.


Life Group Kick-Off - January 12th

Are you looking for a way to connect in our church family this year? Join a Life Group! Meet leaders at our 10 minute parties on Sunday, January 12th in The Studio after each experience to get plugged in.


Launch - January 12th

Launch U exists to invest in our young generation of students, to help grow them as followers of Christ and leaders for His Kingdom. Launch U has age specific leadership groups for 4th - 5th grade, jr. high, and high school.



Lifestream - January 14th

Join thousands of college students each week at Lifestream for an evening of community, worship, and Biblical teaching. With a huge lobby full of friends, food and drinks, Lifestream is a great break in the week for college students to connect with God.

Student Journey - January 15th

Come experience life with other jr. high and high school students and discover what it means to follow Jesus. Student Journey exists to see our campuses transformed one life at a time. No matter where you are from or what you are comfortable with, all students will find a place at Student Journey.


Starting Point - January 19th

Come to Starting Point to discuss the vision and purpose of JourneyChurch and what it means to be a member of the house! Whether you have been attending weeks, months or years, Starting Point is the way learn about the heart of JourneyChurch!
