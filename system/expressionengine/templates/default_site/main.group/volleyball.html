Volleyball

Red, White & Blue Sand Volleyball Tournament

Date: July 11th
Format: 6 vs 6 - must have 2 girls playing at all times

Single Elimination
Time: 7:30pm start, all team members must be present
Where: JourneyChurch.tv Volleyball Courts

Rules: All games are played by rally scoring. This means every mistake is a point for the other team. If you miss a serve, miss a hit, or miss a block, the other team gets a point. All games go to 21. To make sure the game is fair, under weather conditions or sun rays, the teams change sides every 7 points.

Maximum Teams: 20
Registration: Deadline 7:30pm

All team members will be given a red, white or blue bandana for team identification.

Prizes: Golden Volleyball for 1st place team, flags to runner ups
