Welcome to 40 Days!

This is an exciting season for our church as we journey together in a time of spiritual renewal and awakening that will bring us closer to God and each other than ever before. This book is a key part of that journey. In it, you will find a 30 day personal devotional, followed by a guide for embarking on a 10 day period of fasting and prayer.


This 40 day journey will lead us into Easter week, as we join together with thousands to celebrate Christ’s sacrifice and resurrection! I’ve never been more excited about what God is doing, and will do through you. Now let’s start our 40 day journey together!


~ Pastor Clark

What is 40 Days to a Healthier Life?

"40 Days" is a new season of teaching and more. This is more than just a new study... it is a time designed for us as a church to get coser to God than ever before and and to prepare us for the next great move of God in our church. Pastor Clark and the leadership of Journey believe that "40 Days" will be a pivotal turning point in your life and in our church. You do NOT want to miss a single moment of "40 Days."
