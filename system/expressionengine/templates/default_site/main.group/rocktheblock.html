How to Prepare

  • Decide if you want to host or is there a Journey neighbor you can partner with?
  • Pick a date between now and September 6th.
  • Fill out our pre-made invitations. Be sure to include the date, address, time, and RSVP contact.
  • Choose a convenient location, easily accessible and has all the commodities you need



Here are some ideas

  • Pot Luck Cook Out


    Everyone brings a side & you provide the meat

  • B.Y.O.M.


    Bring Your Own Meat & you provide the sides

  • Ice Cream Social


    Homemade ice cream & topping bar

  • You Provide Everything


    Neighbors just show up and enjoy
