Are you in junior high or high school and feeling disconnected to what's going on around you spiritually? Do you want the chance to plug back in, recharge, and become closer to God? We believe that the Stand Up Conference will engage you to not only experience the presence of Jesus but also to put your faith in the path that He has called you to walk on daily.


Our vision is to see students go deeper in their love for Jesus and wider in their relationship with others.


We believe that each student who commits to Standing Up and Standing Out will begin to see their world change in their communities, schools, jobs, sports, and--most importantly--with God.
