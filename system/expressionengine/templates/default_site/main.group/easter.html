JourneyChurch.tv invites all of the Oklahoma City area to join us as we experience Good Friday in the Park at the Myriad Botanical Gardens. Bring your friends, family, blankets and lawn chairs for a time of community at 5:30 PM, followed by an incredible time of worship and remembrance at 7:00 PM. This outdoor event is the perfect opportunity to invite the entire city to experience Jesus’ sacrificial love and forgiveness.
